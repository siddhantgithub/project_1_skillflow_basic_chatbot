# SkillFlow-AI Client - Company Information

## Company Overview

SkillFlow-AI Client is an innovative AI-powered learning platform that helps professionals and students accelerate their career growth through personalized skill development. Founded in 2022, we combine cutting-edge artificial intelligence with expert-curated content to create adaptive learning experiences tailored to each individual's goals and learning style.

Our mission is to democratize access to high-quality education and make continuous learning accessible, engaging, and effective for everyone.

**Headquarters:** San Francisco, CA  
**Founded:** 2022

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

### Account & Billing

**Q: How do I create an account?**  
A: Visit skillflow-ai.com and click "Sign Up" in the top right corner. You can sign up using your email, Google, or GitHub account. It takes less than 2 minutes!

**Q: Can I cancel my subscription anytime?**  
A: Yes! You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.

**Q: Do you offer refunds?**  
A: We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for all paid subscriptions. If you're not satisfied within the first 14 days, contact support@skillflow-ai.com for a full refund.

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**  
A: We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, and Apple Pay.

**Q: Can I switch between monthly and annual billing?**  
A: Yes! You can upgrade to annual billing at any time to save 17%. The change will take effect at your next billing cycle.

### Learning & Courses

**Q: Are the courses self-paced?**  
A: Yes! All courses are completely self-paced. You can learn on your own schedule, pause and resume anytime.

**Q: Do I get certificates?**  
A: Yes, SkillFlow Pro and Teams members receive a certificate of completion for every course they finish. Certificates can be shared on LinkedIn and added to your resume.

**Q: How long do I have access to courses?**  
A: As long as you maintain an active subscription, you have unlimited access to all courses. Downloaded materials remain yours forever.

**Q: Are there prerequisites for courses?**  
A: Each course page lists any prerequisites. We also offer beginner-friendly courses that require no prior experience.

**Q: Can I download course materials?**  
A: Yes! Pro members can download video lectures, code files, and resources for offline learning.

### Technical Support

**Q: What browsers do you support?**  
A: SkillFlow works best on the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We also have mobile apps for iOS and Android.

**Q: I'm having trouble accessing a course. What should I do?**  
A: First, try refreshing your browser and clearing your cache. If the issue persists, contact our support team at support@skillflow-ai.com with details about the course and error message.

**Q: Do you have a mobile app?**  
A: Yes! Our mobile apps are available on the App Store (iOS) and Google Play (Android). You can download courses for offline viewing.

**Q: Can I use SkillFlow on multiple devices?**  
A: Yes! Your account works across all devices. Your progress syncs automatically.

### Teams & Enterprise

**Q: How many users do I need for a Teams account?**  
A: Teams accounts start at 10 users. For larger organizations (100+ users), contact our enterprise sales team for custom pricing.

**Q: Can we create custom content?**  
A: Yes! Teams customers can work with our content team to create custom courses tailored to your organization's needs.

**Q: Do you offer training for administrators?**  
A: Yes, we provide onboarding and training for team administrators to help them get the most out of the platform.
